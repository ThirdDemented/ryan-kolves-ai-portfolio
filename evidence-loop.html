Evidence Drift Lab

A transparent reasoning loop that separates sourced facts from inference, challenges unsupported certainty, rewrites overreaching claims, and learns from human correction.

    System architecture

    Improvement is controlled, observable, and bounded.

    01Extract

    Measures source–claim overlap, unsupported additions, certainty language, and attribution.

    02Evaluate

    Classifies the epistemic status and calculates confidence from visible features.

    03Critique

    Looks for promoted inference, overconfidence, weak provenance, and missing qualification.

    04Optimize

    Produces a bounded revision when the wording outruns the available evidence.

    05Learn

    Human feedback updates local class weights; iteration stops after improvement or three cycles.
